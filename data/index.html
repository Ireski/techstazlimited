Course Overview

What You'll Learn

A complete roadmap from data beginner to confident analyst — no prior coding or statistics experience needed.

Data Foundations

Understand what data analytics is, how data flows in organisations, and the key concepts behind data-driven decision-making.

Week 1

Excel & Spreadsheets

Master formulas, pivot tables, VLOOKUP, conditional formatting, and charts. The most essential tool in every analyst's toolkit.

Week 2–3

SQL & Databases

Write queries to extract, filter, join, and aggregate data from relational databases. Query real datasets from day one.

Week 4–5

Python for Data

Use Pandas and NumPy to clean, transform, and analyse datasets. Automate repetitive tasks that would take hours in Excel.

Week 6–8

Data Visualisation

Build compelling charts with Matplotlib, Seaborn, and Power BI. Tell clear data stories that non-technical stakeholders understand.

Week 9–10

Capstone Project

Analyse a real-world dataset end-to-end — clean, explore, visualise, and present your findings in a professional dashboard.
