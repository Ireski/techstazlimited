Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've answered the most common ones here to help you move forward with clarity and confidence.

Do I need a tech background to enroll?

No. Our programmes are designed for beginners and career switchers. You can start with little or no prior experience — we guide you step-by-step, equipping you with practical, job-ready skills.

How long are the training programmes?

Programme durations vary depending on the course and your chosen learning schedule. Contact us to get specific timelines for the course you're interested in.

Are the classes online or physical?

We offer flexible learning options — both online and onsite. Whether you prefer remote learning or a classroom environment, you can participate from wherever is most convenient for you.

Can I pay in installments?

Yes. We offer flexible payment plans to make learning accessible without financial strain. Reach out to discuss the options available for your chosen programme.

How do I register?
  1. Speak with our representative via phone or WhatsApp
  2. Make your payment (full or first installment)
  3. Fill in the enrollment form
  4. Get onboarded and start learning
Will I receive a certificate after training?

Yes. Students receive a certificate upon successful completion of the programme, which you can share with employers and on LinkedIn.
